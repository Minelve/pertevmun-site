GA:3 SOCHUM

Agenda item: Combating the rise of neo-nazism and far-right extremism in the digital age

The rise of neo-Nazism and far-right extremism poses a serious threat to democratic values, human rights, and global security. In the digital age, extremist ideologies spread speedily, fueling hate speech, racism, and violence. Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Committee, affectionately known as SOCHUM,will address the root causes of this resurgence and strategies to counter extremist movements while safeguarding fundamental freedoms.

In this edition of PertevMUN, the General Assembly Third Committee: SOCHUM invites delegates to engage in diplomatic discussions, propose effective policies, and strengthen international cooperation to obstruct rising neo-Nazism and extremism worldwide.
