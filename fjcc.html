Agenda Item: War of Zaun and Piltover

The rivalry between Zaun and Piltover has reached a breaking point. On one side, Piltover, the City of Progress, thrives on innovation and Hextech, striving for stability and prosperity. On the other, Zaun, the Undercity, fights for survival, fueled by resentment, shimmer, and rebellion against Piltover’s oppressive rule.

As tensions escalate, powerful figures from both cities must navigate political intrigue, technological advancements, and rising conflict. Will Piltover maintain its dominance, or will Zaun rise from the shadows to claim its place?
