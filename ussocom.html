USSOCOM

Agenda Item: Retaliation for September 11 attacks.

''These acts shattered steel, but they cannot dent the steel of American resolve" -Former President, George W. Bush

United States Special Operations Command (USSOCOM) develops, and employs, the world's finest Special Operation Forces to conduct global special operations and activities as part of it's aim of bringing peace, freedom, justice and security to the world that threatens the stability of its people.
This committee will take a hold of the fate of the United States of America from the moment the Twin Towers are hit, until the delegates deem fit.

PertevMUN'25 participants will take on the roles of high-ranking American generals as they seek to end terrorism and exact revenge.
