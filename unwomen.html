UNWOMEN

Agenda Item: Social, political, and economic obstacles to girls' education in the Middle East

This committee is dedicated to tackling the deep-rooted challenges that prevent young girls in the Middle East from accessing quality education. Under the guidance of UNWOMEN, delegates will collaborate to examine the social, political, and economic factors that limit educational opportunities, including gender-based discrimination, financial hardships, cultural norms, and conflict-driven disruptions. Bringing together a diverse group of delegates, this committee will explore policy innovations, educational reforms, and community-driven solutions to ensure that every girl has the right to learn, grow, and shape her own future. This is a crucial opportunity to drive meaningful change and promote equity in education on a global scale. Let’s rewrite the future of girls in Middle East!
